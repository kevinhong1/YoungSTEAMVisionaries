Become a STEAM Mentor

Inspire young minds and shape the future of STEAM education while developing valuable leadership skills

Are you passionate about science, technology, engineering, arts, or mathematics? Do you enjoy working with children? At Young STEAM Visionaries, we're looking for dedicated high school students who want to make STEAM education accessible and exciting for young learners.

If you're a high school student with strong STEAM skills and a desire to inspire the next generation of innovators, we'd love to have you join our team. Fill out the application below, and we'll get back to you soon.

Your Responsibilities

STEAM Sessions

Lead engaging virtual STEAM activities and experiments for young learners

Session Preparation

Prepare materials and review STEAM concepts for interactive learning sessions

Communication

Maintain clear communication with students, parents, and our team

Creative Teaching

Develop fun and interactive ways to explain STEAM concepts to children

What's in it for You

Leadership Experience

Gain valuable teaching and leadership experience that stands out on college applications

Flexible Schedule

Choose mentoring hours that work with your academic and extracurricular commitments

Community Impact

Make a real difference by inspiring young minds in STEAM education

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in high school
  • Strong performance in STEAM subjects
  • Experience with hands-on STEAM projects
  • Reliable internet connection for virtual sessions
  • Commitment to at least 1 hour per week
  • Passion for teaching and working with children
