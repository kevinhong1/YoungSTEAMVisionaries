About Young STEAM Visionaries

Empowering K-8 students with free worksheets & peer tutoring

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of high-achieving high school students who excel in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM) subjects and more.

Driven by a passion for learning and a desire to give back, we founded Young STEAM Visionaries to ensure everybody has access to engaging resources and personalised support.

Our free printable worksheets, hands-on activities, and one-on-one peer tutoring sessions help K-8 learners build confidence, master core concepts, and provide students with a solid foundation for learning.

When you download a worksheet or book a tutoring session, you’re connecting with peers who are eager to help students thrive.
